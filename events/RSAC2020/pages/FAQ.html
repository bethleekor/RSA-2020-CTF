1. What is this?

    This is a Capture The Flag (CTF) game that is designed to give you hands on experience with application security by challenging you to exploit vulnerabilities in an intentionally insecure application.

  2. How do I check my score and where I stand?

    Got to the Scoreboard tab to check out every participant's score.

  3. How to I register for this CTF?

    Go to Register. Register an account by entering your details.

  4. What happens after I register?

    After you register, we will create an insecure web application for you.
